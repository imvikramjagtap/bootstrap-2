2 Free Ways to Use iPad as Second Monitor for PC

written by Mehvish January 2, 2022

Using the Sidecar feature, Apple provides a native way to use the iPad as a secondary display for macOS. However, not everyone owns a Mac. Fortunately, you can use an iPad as a second monitor on a Windows PC for free. You’ll need to take the help of third-party apps. We have covered two apps here. One can connect wirelessly and the other requires a wire. Let’s take a look at both methods.

1. Use Cable to connect iPad as Second Monitor with PC

Splashtop Wired XDisplay is one of the best free apps to use iPad as a second monitor for a Windows PC or Mac. As the name suggests, you need to connect PC and iPad using a lightning cable. Also, this app requires iTunes to be installed on your PC for best performance.
